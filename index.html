About Us

S K P VIDYA VIHAR BANKA

S. K. P. VIDIYA VIHAR, RAJPUR was established in 1989 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in AMARPUR block of BANKA district of Bihar. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Private in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road.
